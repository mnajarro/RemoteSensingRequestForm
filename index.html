REMOTE SENSING PROGRAM

How to Obtain Imagery at USAID:

  1. Review and agree to the Data Use Agreement on using NextView imagery.
  2. Work with the project's Contract Officer's Representative (COR) or Agreement Officer's Representative (AOR) to fill out the Request Form and submit the area of interest you need imagery for.
  3. Contact the GeoCenter Remote Sensing Program at remotesensing@usaid.gov to confirm the request and to receive an estimated delivery date.
